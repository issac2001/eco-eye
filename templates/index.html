ECO - EYE : SPATIAL SOLUTIONS FOR
ENVIRONMENTAL MANAGEMENT

Our forest fire detection system is equipped with advanced capabilities to detect both fires and smoke. Leveraging cutting-edge technology, it provides real-time alerts for fire outbreaks and smoke detection, enabling swift response and mitigation efforts. With its comprehensive monitoring, it plays a vital role in safeguarding forests and surrounding communities from the devastating impacts of wildfires.

Our visualization platform offers a comprehensive set of features to analyze forest cover change year by year. It enables users to and understand the spatial and temporal patterns of forest change, predict future changes, and identify areas susceptible to high deforestation rates. With its predictive capabilities, it can be used to make informed decisions for sustainable environmental management.
